Meet the Founder

Meet Max Joukovski

Hey, I'm Max. I started this when I was 16, junior year of high school. It began as calculus tutoring in Templeton, California, and somewhere along the way it turned into something that now works with hundreds of families across Texas.

The SAT took me years to figure out, so I care about two things here. First, make prep clear and approachable for every student. Second, show other students that they can build something of their own too.

Right now I'm a junior studying Finance at UT Austin, and in 2027 I'll be joining Morgan Stanley in New York as an investment banking analyst.
