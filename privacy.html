Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Max Potential Learning, LLC ("Max Potential Learning," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or use our SAT preparation services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as a student or parent name, email address, phone number, and details related to SAT preparation (for example, practice test results or diagnostic responses). We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as browser type and general usage data, to help us improve the website.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, schedule, and improve our SAT preparation services.
  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with students and families.
  • To review diagnostic and practice results and provide recommendations.
  • To send relevant updates you have requested.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, or when required by law. Any such providers are expected to handle information responsibly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are often used by high school students with the involvement of a parent or guardian. We encourage parents and guardians to participate in and supervise their student's use of our services. If you believe a minor has provided information without appropriate consent, please contact us so we can address it.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us using the details below. You may also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
